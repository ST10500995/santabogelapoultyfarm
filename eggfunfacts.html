Egg Fun Facts

Learn interesting and fun facts about eggs and chickens while enjoying some egg-cellent humour!

Interesting Egg Facts

  • Why did the egg get thrown out of class? Because it kept cracking jokes!
  • Chickens sometimes lay eggs with double yolks — it’s like finding a twin surprise inside!
  • An egg shell can have up to 17,000 tiny pores.
  • The oldest recorded egg-laying chicken lived to be 16 years old.
  • Some hens lay eggs too large for standard cartons.
  • In some countries, people enjoy egg-rolling contests.
  • Hummingbirds lay the smallest eggs while ostriches lay the largest eggs.
  • The world record for balancing eggs on end is 888 eggs.
  • Why did the egg sit on the computer? To hatch a new idea!
