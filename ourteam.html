Meet Our Team

A dedicated family working together to deliver fresh and high-quality poultry products.

One Big Family

At Santabogela Poultry Farm, we are more than just a team — we are one big family built on values of care, respect, teamwork, and dedication.

The passion and commitment we show every day begins with the way we care for our chickens and the farm environment. We believe that love and care are reflected in the quality of the eggs we produce.

Every egg is carefully collected, inspected, and packaged to ensure freshness and customer satisfaction for families across South Africa.

Our Team Values

  • Commitment to quality and excellence
  • Respect for customers and community
  • Ethical and responsible farming practices
  • Care and compassion for animals
  • Teamwork and professionalism

Our Farm Family

Animal Care and Sustainability

Our commitment to animal welfare extends beyond caring for chickens. We strive to create a healthy and sustainable environment for all animals on the farm.

By maintaining ethical farming practices, we improve product quality while supporting environmental sustainability and community wellbeing.

Our Vision for the Future

As Santabogela Poultry Farm continues to grow, we remain focused on innovation, sustainability, and delivering high-quality poultry products to customers nationwide.
