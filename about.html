About Us

The Santabogela Poultry Farm opened in 2021 and is located in Winterveld, Gauteng, South Africa.
It is situated approximately 30–35 miles (40–50 km) northwest of Pretoria, within the City of Tshwane
Metropolitan Municipality, and near the border of the North West Province, close to towns such as Mabopane and Soshanguve.
The name Santabogela is derived from my late grandfather, Santabogela Carpos Mashile.
He was a self-driven individual, often described as selfless and oriented toward family and community.
He was always supportive and welcoming to those around him. His love for backyard free-range chickens was evident,
and he would sometimes ask me to assist him in administering medication to the chickens and trimming their wings.

This is where my passion for chickens began, which inspired the decision to name the farm after him, as we want to share
warmth and community love with both the residents of Wintervend and our customers from all corners of the country.
