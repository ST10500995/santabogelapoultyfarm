About Santabogela Poultry Farm

Built from passion, family values, and a love for poultry farming.

Our Story

Santabogela Poultry Farm was established in 2021 and is located in Winterveld, Gauteng, South Africa.

The farm is situated northwest of Pretoria within the City of Tshwane Metropolitan Municipality, near Mabopane and Soshanguve.

The name Santabogela was inspired by my late grandfather, Santabogela Carpos Mashile.

He was known as a caring, selfless, and hardworking individual who valued family and community. His love for backyard free-range chickens inspired my passion for poultry farming from a young age.

I often assisted him with caring for the chickens, administering medication, and helping around the yard. These experiences inspired the creation of Santabogela Poultry Farm.

Today, our farm continues his legacy by providing fresh, high-quality eggs while sharing warmth, care, and community love with customers across South Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fresh and affordable eggs while maintaining excellent farming practices, customer service, and product quality.

Our Core Values

  • Quality and freshness
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Community support
  • Hard work and dedication
  • Clean and safe farming practices
